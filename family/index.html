For Families

Family Resources

Everything you need to stay connected, informed, and involved in your child's education at Old Town Academy.

At OTA, families are not simply spectators. You are partners. From the daily homework routine to campus events and governance decisions, our community works best when parents and caregivers are active participants in school life. This page is your central hub for calendars, policies, digital tools, and everything else you need throughout the year.

If you ever have questions that are not answered here, please reach out to the front office at (619) 574-6225 or email info@oldtownacademy.org. We are always happy to help.

Resources and Tools

School Calendar

Upcoming events, important dates, holidays, and school-wide activities for the current academic year.

Student and Parent Handbook

Attendance policies, dress code, behavior expectations, communication guidelines, and health and safety information.

PowerSchool

Check your child's grades, attendance records, and academic progress in real time. Log in with your parent account.

Canvas LMS

Access class assignments, learning materials, and teacher communications through our learning management system.

Uniform Policy

Details on the OTA dress code, approved uniform items, and spirit wear days throughout the year.

Contact the Office

Reach our front office for attendance questions, early pickup, schedule changes, or general inquiries.

Stay in the loop.

Follow Old Town Academy on social media for campus updates, event reminders, student highlights, and community news throughout the school year.
